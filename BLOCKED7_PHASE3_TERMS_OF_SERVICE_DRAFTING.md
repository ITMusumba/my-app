FARM TRANSACTION RECORDING PLATFORM

In these Terms, unless the context requires otherwise:

"Administrator" means a user authorized to manage system settings and user roles.

"Ledger Entry" means a system-generated record reflecting a transaction event.

"Pilot Mode" means a system state in which transactional functions may be restricted.

"Platform" means the software system used to record and coordinate agricultural transactions.

"Transaction Record" means a non-editable, system-generated record identified by a Unique Transaction Identifier.

"UTID" means a Unique Transaction Identifier generated by the Platform.

"User" means any person authorized to access the Platform.

Headings are for convenience only and do not affect interpretation

Singular includes plural and vice versa

References to law include amendments and re-enactments

3.1 Transaction Recording System

The Platform is a transaction-recording and coordination system designed to:

Record transaction-related events

Generate auditable transaction records

Provide evidentiary traceability

3.2 Exclusions

The Platform:

Is not a bank

Is not a financial institution

Does not provide payment services

Does not issue electronic money

Does not hold funds on behalf of Users

Does not act as agent, trustee, or fiduciary

Nothing in these Terms shall be construed to create a banking, fiduciary, escrow, or agency relationship.

Users must:

Be at least eighteen (18) years old

Have legal capacity to contract

Use the Platform only for lawful purposes

4.2 Account Roles

Each User is assigned a specific role. Rights and permissions are limited strictly to the assigned role.

Role assignment is controlled by an Administrator.

5. TRANSACTION RECORDS AND EVIDENTIARY STATUS
5.1 Transaction Records

Each transaction recorded on the Platform generates a UTID and corresponding Transaction Record.

5.2 Legal Effect

Transaction Records constitute prima facie evidence of:

The occurrence of recorded events

The timestamp of such events

The parties involved as recorded

Records are auditable, non-editable, and persist indefinitely, subject to lawful retention requirements.

5.3 No Substitution

Platform records do not replace external contracts or statutory requirements.

6.1 Pilot Mode Operation

The Platform may operate in Pilot Mode, during which certain transactions may be restricted or blocked.

Users acknowledge that:

Pilot Mode restrictions are intentional

No liability arises from Pilot Mode restrictions

Administrators may enable or disable Pilot Mode

7. FEES, LEDGER ENTRIES, AND NO CUSTODY
7.1 Ledger Entries

Ledger Entries reflect recorded events only.

They:

Are not bank balances

Do not represent deposits

Do not accrue interest

Are not protected by deposit insurance

7.2 Fees

Fees may be recorded and deducted according to platform rules.

8. DATA PROTECTION AND PRIVACY
8.1 Legal Compliance

The Platform processes personal data in accordance with the Data Protection and Privacy Act, 2019 (Uganda).

8.2 Consent

By using the Platform, Users consent to lawful data processing necessary for platform operation.

9. SUSPENSION AND TERMINATION
9.1 Suspension

Accounts may be suspended for:

Breach of these Terms

Misuse of the Platform

Legal or regulatory requirements

9.2 Termination

Termination does not result in deletion of Transaction Records.

10. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY

To the fullest extent permitted by Ugandan law:

The Platform is provided "as is"

No liability for indirect or consequential loss

No exclusion of liability for fraud or criminal conduct

11. INDEMNITY

Users indemnify the Platform against losses arising from misuse or unlawful use.

12. AMENDMENTS

Terms may be updated. Material changes require renewed User acceptance.

13. GOVERNING LAW AND JURISDICTION

These Terms are governed by the laws of the Republic of Uganda.

Courts of competent jurisdiction in Uganda shall have exclusive jurisdiction.

14. ENTIRE AGREEMENT

These Terms constitute the entire agreement between the User and the Platform.

15. CONTACT AND NOTICES

Official notices shall be issued through the Platform or recorded communication channels.

16. ACCEPTANCE RECORD

Acceptance of these Terms shall be:

Explicit

Versioned

Timestamped

Stored as a non-repudiable record
