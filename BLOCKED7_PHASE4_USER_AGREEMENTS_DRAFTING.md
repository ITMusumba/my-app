### 2.2 Role-Specific User Agreements Text

```
ROLE-SPECIFIC USER AGREEMENTS
FARM TRANSACTION RECORDING PLATFORM

Effective Date: [TO BE SET UPON GO-LIVE AUTHORIZATION]
Version: 1.0
Governing Law: Republic of Uganda

1. PURPOSE AND STRUCTURE

These Role-Specific User Agreements ("Agreements") apply in addition to the Terms of Service.

Each User agrees to one or more of the following agreements based strictly on their assigned role.

No role may exercise rights or assume obligations outside its designated agreement.

2. FARMER USER AGREEMENT
2.1 Role Description

A Farmer User is a producer of agricultural commodities who uses the Platform to record delivery, storage, or sale-related transaction events.

2.2 Permitted Use

The Farmer User may:

Record delivery and transaction events

View transaction records relating to their produce

Rely on UTID-backed records as proof of recorded events

2.3 Acknowledgements

The Farmer User acknowledges that:

The Platform does not purchase produce

The Platform does not guarantee payment

Records reflect events, not legal settlement

2.4 Representations

The Farmer User represents that:

Produce recorded belongs to them or is lawfully controlled

All submitted information is accurate

Use complies with Ugandan law

2.5 Evidentiary Consent

The Farmer User consents to Transaction Records being used as prima facie evidence in dispute resolution, administrative, or judicial proceedings.

3. TRADER / BUYER USER AGREEMENT
3.1 Role Description

A Trader or Buyer User records purchase-related transaction events and interacts with recorded inventory.

3.2 Permitted Use

The Trader or Buyer User may:

Initiate purchase-related transaction records

View transaction history relevant to their role

Rely on records for reconciliation and audit

3.3 Prohibited Conduct

The Trader or Buyer User shall not:

Circumvent platform controls

Attempt to alter records

Treat ledger entries as bank balances

3.4 Financial Disclaimer

The Trader or Buyer User acknowledges:

No funds are held by the Platform

Ledger entries are not deposits

External payment obligations remain independent

4. STORAGE OPERATOR AGREEMENT
4.1 Role Description

A Storage Operator User records storage-related events, including intake, holding, and release of goods.

4.2 Duties

The Storage Operator User shall:

Accurately record storage events

Maintain integrity of records

Act in good faith

4.3 No Custody by Platform

The Storage Operator User acknowledges that:

Custody of goods is physical and external

The Platform does not assume custody or liability

5. ADMINISTRATOR AGREEMENT
5.1 Role Description

An Administrator manages system configuration and user roles.

5.2 Elevated Responsibilities

Administrators shall:

Act lawfully and proportionately

Maintain audit integrity

Avoid misuse of elevated privileges

5.3 Accountability

All administrative actions are:

Logged

Timestamped

Attributable

Subject to audit

5.4 Legal Consequences

Misuse of administrative authority may result in:

Immediate suspension

Civil liability

Criminal referral where applicable

6. SYSTEM OPERATOR AGREEMENT
6.1 Role Description

The System Operator has ultimate authority over platform governance.

6.2 Authority Scope

The System Operator may:

Authorize execution and go-live

Engage legal and technical oversight

Define system policies

6.3 Legal Responsibility

The System Operator affirms:

Compliance with Ugandan law

Accuracy of legal characterizations

Maintenance of auditability

7. ROLE ASSIGNMENT AND LIMITATION
7.1 Assignment Control

Roles are:

Explicitly assigned

Not self-selectable

Logged and auditable

7.2 Limitation

No User may act outside their assigned role.

8. DATA, RECORDS, AND EVIDENCE
8.1 Record Integrity

All Users acknowledge:

Records are immutable

UTIDs uniquely identify events

Records persist independent of account status

8.2 Evidentiary Use

All Users consent to use of records as evidence, subject to Ugandan law.

9. TERMINATION AND SURVIVAL

Termination of access:

Does not delete records

Does not negate evidentiary value

Does not extinguish legal obligations

10. GOVERNING LAW AND JURISDICTION

These Agreements are governed by the laws of Uganda.

Courts of competent jurisdiction in Uganda have exclusive jurisdiction.

11. ACCEPTANCE AND RECORDING

Acceptance of the applicable Role-Specific Agreement is:

Explicit

Role-scoped

Versioned

Timestamped

Stored as a non-repudiable system record
```
